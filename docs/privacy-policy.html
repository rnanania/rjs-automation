Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

DIANET TECHNOLOGIES LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates RJS Automation and is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with us by phone, SMS, or through our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you place an order or opt in to our messaging service:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Order details and transaction history
  • Communication preferences (opt-in / opt-out status)
  • IP address and browser type when visiting our website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and confirm your orders
  • Send real-time order status updates via SMS
  • Deliver promotional offers, coupons, and special announcements to subscribed customers
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our products and services

3. SMS Messaging & Consent

By providing your phone number and agreeing to receive messages from RJS Automation, you consent to receive SMS communications including:

  • Order confirmations and status updates
  • Delivery or pickup notifications
  • Promotional offers and marketing messages

Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-In: Customers provide verbal consent when placing an order by phone. A follow-up confirmation text is sent, and the customer must reply YES to complete their opt-in.

Opt-Out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed and will receive no further messages.

Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at rohit.nanania@gmail.com.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as Twilio for SMS delivery) solely to operate our services, subject to confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Opt-out records are retained to ensure we honor your preferences.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.
